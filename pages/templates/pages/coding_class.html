Coding Classes

Our program begins with coding classes to equip enrollees with marketable skills leading to certification in six areas, including Front End Developer and Full Stack Developer.

  • The program is self-paced with highly individualized instructor support. Students who can master the content more quickly can complete certification more rapidly than others. Those who need more time can have more time.

  • Instruction is provided through six modules, each leading to certification. At the end of the third module, participants earn Front End Developer certification.

  • Once a participant has the Front End Developer certificate he can begin the work experience component of the program, earning money and valuable experience while still working to complete the rest of the coding modules.

  • Meeting the needs of your organization and your clients is our goal. The coding instructional component can be offered in six, twelve, or eighteen months, based on the setting and structure of your existing programs.

  • The course can be offered in an incarceration or community-based setting, including appropriate security features for each setting.
