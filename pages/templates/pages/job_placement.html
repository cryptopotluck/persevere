Job Placement Services

Persevere doesn't leave participants on their own to find jobs. We provide job placement services to match each participant with a well-paying job in the technology industry.

  • Before participants complete the program, Persevere begins the process of finding them meaningful, well-paying jobs in the field.

  • An Employment Specialist works with each participant to match him with a good job and help him get it.

  • The Employment Specialist also helps participants develop resumes and interviewing skills so they can be successful with the current effort to get a job and efforts in the future.
